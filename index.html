Iberian Lynx

Level 1

The world's most endangered feline species, with distinctive leopard-like spotted fur and black ear tufts. Native to southwestern Spain, particularly in the Doñana and Sierra Morena regions.

Critical Threats

Habitat loss due to agriculture and urban development, reduction in rabbit populations (their main prey), road accidents, and illegal hunting. Climate change is exacerbating these threats.

Protection Efforts

Captive breeding programs, rabbit population restoration, wildlife corridors, and road mortality reduction initiatives supported by EU LIFE program funding.
